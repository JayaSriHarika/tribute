Steve Jobs was an American business magnate, industrial designer, and inventor who co-founded Apple Inc. His groundbreaking work in personal computers, mobile devices, and digital media revolutionized several industries. Known for his perfectionism, visionary leadership, and innovative products like the iPhone, iPad, and Macintosh, Jobs has left an indelible mark on the world.
